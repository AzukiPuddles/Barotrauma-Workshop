A recreation of the parallel talents mod with support for many job mods, this one primarily designed for [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2526236489]Jobs Extended[/url]. As such this will only support mods that do not overlap with it.

What this mod does:
Allows you to freely switch between trees to spend your talent points on.
Allows you to take all talents in a tree, instead of having one per tier.


While there are no issues running this even if you use no job mods, I recommend using the original if that's your plan. But if you want a shorter mod list and/or support for jobs that don't have patches this is the mod for you.
Load order generally doesn't matter unless something overrides the trees already, or unless stated.
